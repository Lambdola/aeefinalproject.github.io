4. Under-Graduate Programme

MINIMUM DEGREE REQUIREMENTS

a) The following regulations govern the conditions for the award of degree under the 5-year programmes:
i. All courses taken at 100 - 500 level are to be use in the computtaion of the class of the degree.

ii. All compulsory courses must be taken and passed.

iii. Approved units from the General Studies Programme courses must be taken before graduation.

iv.The minimum number of course units for the award of honors degree, includding all the compulsory courses shall be 120.

v. The "Pass" degree shall no longer be awarded.

b) The CGPA of transfer undergraduates shall be calculated on the basis of all courses taken within the programme
i.Candidates who transferred to 200 level for the award of Honours degree shall be required to pass a minimum of 96 units before graduation.

ii. Candidates who transfered to 300 level for the award of Honours degree must pass a minimum of 72 units before graduation.

iii. For other undergraduates admitted to 100 level, the following regulations govern the conditions for the award of their degree with respect to grading and class of degree:

GRADING
Aggregate Mark Grade Point
A 70 - 100 4

CLASS OF DEGREE
C.G.P.A Class of Degree
3.50 - 4.00 First Class Honours
3.00 - 3.49 Second Class Honours (Upper Division)
2.00 - 2.99 Second Class Honours (Lower Division)
1.00 - 1.99 Third Class Honours

Note: For the purpose of determination of the class of degree, the G.P.A or C.G.P.A (as the case may be) shall be expressed correct to two decimal places and pass mark is now 45% NOT 40%


Those admitted through direct entry into any level whose result computation is based on the old system are exempted from the 4.00 grade point system.
