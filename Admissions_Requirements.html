3. Admissions Requirements

In addition to an acceptable pass in Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME), candidates must have:
a) Senior Secondary School Certificate (SSCE) credit passes in English Language, Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry and any one at one sitting or any two at two sittings from the following subjects: Biology, Agricultural Science, Technical Drawing, EConomics, Geography, Metal/Woodwork and Further Mathematics or;

b) West African School Certificate (WASC) / General Certificate of EDucation (GCE) Ordinary Level credit passes in English Language, Mthematics, Physics, and Chemistry and any one at one sitting or any two at two sittings from the following subjects: Biology, Agricultural Science, Technical Drawing, EConomics, Geography, Metal/Woodwork and Further Mathematics
