5. Post-Graduate Programme

Post-graduate training programmes commenced in the department in th 1970s, and the first Ph.D. was produced in 1984. Currently, the Department still offers M.Sc., M.Phil. and Ph.D. training programes in all fields of Agricultural and Environmental Enginnerring.

The area of specialization in the Agricultural and Environmental Engineering Programme are:
i. Farm power and Machinery

ii. Crop Processing and Storage

iii. Soil and Water Conservation

iv. Farm Structures and Environment

v. Environmental Engineering

Rural Electrification and Utilization

Biomass Energy Conversion
